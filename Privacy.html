YOGRAME Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 19, 2026

YOGRAME respects your privacy. This application allows users connected to the same Wi-Fi network to exchange messages and files directly without relying on external servers.

Information We Collect

The application may access the following information only to provide its core functionality:

  • Username and optional profile picture
  • Messages and shared files stored locally on your device
  • Local network information to discover nearby devices
  • Notification permissions to alert you about new messages

How We Use Information

  • Discover devices connected to the same Wi-Fi network
  • Send and receive messages and files
  • Store conversations locally on your device
  • Display notifications

Data Sharing

YOGRAME does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party.

Internet and Servers

YOGRAME does not require external servers for messaging. Communication occurs directly between devices on the same local network.

Data Security

Messages and files remain on your device and are transmitted only to selected devices within your local network.

Children's Privacy

YOGRAME does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
